FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Use these FAQs as a launch-ready support page for your air purifier independent station.

How do I choose the right air purifier for my room?

Start with your room size, then compare CADR, filter type, noise level and usage concern. Bedrooms usually need quieter models, while living rooms often need higher airflow.

Which AERLUNE model is best for a small bedroom or desk?

LUNA Mini is the most compact option and works well for bedside tables, nurseries, small bedrooms and desktop use.

Which model is better for pet homes?

AERLUNE S1 and Pro 600 are the best starting points for pet homes because they are positioned for larger spaces and pair well with HEPA + activated carbon messaging.

What is the difference between the S1 and Pro 600?

S1 is the balanced everyday family model for medium rooms, while Pro 600 is positioned for larger, more open spaces and stronger airflow needs.

What does the H2 model do?

AERLUNE H2 is a purifier + humidifier concept model, suitable for shoppers who want air cleaning plus additional moisture support in drier indoor climates.

How often should I replace the filter?

Filter timing depends on pollution level, runtime and model. A practical starting point is every 3–6 months, with reminders or subscription options improving repeat purchase.

Can I subscribe to replacement filters?

Yes. The site is structured to support filter subscriptions and recurring replenishment offers, which you can connect to your ecommerce platform before launch.

Are the specifications on this benchmark site final?

No. This website package is a branded template and the room-size, CADR, noise, certification and warranty details should be replaced with your actual product data before launch.

Can this site be used for both B2C and B2B?

Yes. The structure supports direct-to-consumer storytelling while also adding OEM / ODM and wholesale inquiry flows for distributors, Amazon sellers and private-label brands.

Do you support OEM / ODM and private label?

Yes. The OEM page is already included in the site package and can be expanded with your MOQ, packaging options, certification files, lead time and market-specific compliance details.

Can I deploy this site to GitHub Pages?

Yes. This package is static and GitHub Pages friendly. Upload the extracted files to your repository root and enable Pages in repository settings.

Can I replace the generated images with my own photos later?

Absolutely. All images are stored in the assets/images folder, so you can replace them at any time and keep the page structure unchanged.

Still need help?

If your question is about model selection, wholesale pricing, shipping or filter compatibility, send a message from the contact page and connect that form to your CRM before going live.
