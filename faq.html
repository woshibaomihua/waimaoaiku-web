OEM / ODM AIR PURIFIER SOURCING

Frequently Asked Questions — Air Purifier OEM & B2B Sourcing

Everything buyers, distributors, and Amazon sellers need to know about sourcing OEM/ODM air purifiers from LYL Clean Air.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for OEM air purifiers?

The standard MOQ is 200 units per model. For new customers placing their first trial order, a lower MOQ of 50–100 units may be negotiated depending on the model and customization requirements.

Can LYL Clean Air manufacture air purifiers under my private label?

Yes. LYL Clean Air offers full OEM and ODM services including logo printing, custom packaging design, color customization, and accessory branding. We support distributors, Amazon sellers, and brand owners worldwide.

What certifications do LYL Clean Air purifiers carry?

Our air purifiers hold CE, FCC, RoHS, and CARB certifications suitable for sale in Europe, North America, and global markets. ETL and UL certifications are available on select models upon request.

What is the typical lead time for an OEM air purifier order?

Standard production lead time is 30–45 days after order confirmation and sample approval. Customized packaging may add 7–10 days. Rush production can be arranged for an additional fee.

What HEPA filter grades are available?

We supply air purifiers with H11, H13, and H14 HEPA filters. H13 is the most popular choice for consumer and commercial markets. H14 is available for medical or cleanroom-grade applications.

Can replacement filters be supplied alongside the air purifier order?

Yes. We can plan replacement filter inventory together with your air purifier order to support after-sales programs and encourage repeat purchases from your end customers.

Does LYL Clean Air support dropshipping or small-batch wholesale?

We focus on B2B wholesale and OEM orders rather than individual dropshipping. For Amazon FBA sellers and distributors requiring small trial batches, we offer flexible starter programs starting from 50 units.

What is CADR and how does it affect product selection?

CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate) measures the volume of filtered air delivered per minute. Higher CADR means faster air cleaning for larger rooms. LYL Clean Air offers models ranging from 100 to 450 CFM CADR to match different room-size market segments.

Can I visit the factory before placing an order?

Yes. Factory visits and video audits are both welcome. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to schedule. We can also arrange product samples and a virtual factory tour for overseas buyers.

What is the MOQ for private label air purifiers?

The MOQ for private label (OEM) orders is 200 units per model. For first-time buyers or trial orders, we may accept 50–100 units depending on the model selected and the scope of customization. Contact us with your target country and quantity for a fast confirmation.

Can I order 50–100 pcs for a trial run?

Yes. We offer flexible starter programs for new buyers. A trial order of 50–100 units is available on select models, ideal for Amazon FBA sellers, distributors testing a new product line, or brand owners confirming market fit before scaling.

Which air purifier model is best for pet odor?

We recommend models with H13 True HEPA + activated carbon composite filters, which effectively capture pet dander and neutralize odors. Our Square Bedroom Air Purifier and Compact Desktop HEPA models are popular for pet-focused product lines. Share your target room size and channel, and we will recommend the right CADR range.

Do you provide replacement filter packaging for private label?

Yes. We can supply replacement filters with your branded packaging — including custom box design, logo printing, and multi-pack configurations. Planning filter inventory alongside your initial air purifier order allows you to build a repeat-purchase program that increases customer lifetime value.

Can you support Amazon FBA air purifier sellers?

Yes. We regularly work with Amazon FBA sellers across the US, UK, Germany, Japan, and Canada. We can assist with FBA-compliant packaging dimensions, carton labeling, FNSKU sticker placement, and certification documentation. CE, FCC, RoHS, and CARB reports are available for key markets.

Which certifications are needed for USA, Europe, and the Middle East?

For the USA: FCC (radio/electrical), CARB (California air quality), and ETL or UL are commonly required. For Europe: CE marking (covers safety, EMC, RoHS). For the Middle East: SASO (Saudi Arabia) and G-Mark (Gulf countries) may be required depending on the product. Our standard models carry CE, FCC, and RoHS. ETL, UL, and regional certifications are available on request.

Share your logo file and packaging direction to get started.
