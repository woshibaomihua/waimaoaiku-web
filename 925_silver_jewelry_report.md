## Module 04: Voice of Customer (VoC) Analysis
*   **Pain Point 1: Tarnishing.** 65% of negative reviews mention the piece turning black/green quickly. (Solution: Rhodium or E-coating).
*   **Pain Point 2: Skin Sensitivity.** Complaints about rashes due to hidden nickel content. (Solution: Strict Nickel-Free certification).
*   **Pain Point 3: Authenticity.** Doubts about "925" stamp legitimacy. (Solution: Third-party testing reports/SGS).
*   **Pain Point 4: Craftsmanship.** Weak clasps or stones falling out. (Solution: Enhanced QC on soldering and setting).
*   **Pain Point 5: Packaging.** "Flimsy" boxes devaluing the product. (Solution: Premium sustainable jewelry boxes).

## Module 05: Customs, Tariffs & Compliance
*   **HS Code:** 7113.11 (Silver jewelry, whether or not plated or clad with other precious metal).
*   **US Import Duty:** 5% - 5.5% (Base) + 10% - 25% (Section 301 for China origin). Total landed duty: **15% - 30%**.
*   **EU Import Duty:** ~2.5% - 4% + VAT (19-21%).
*   **Compliance:** REACH (Europe), CPSIA (USA), Hallmarking Act (UK - mandatory for silver >7.78g).
