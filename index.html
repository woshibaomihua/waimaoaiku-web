B2B BUYER FAQ

Common Questions from Brands and Wholesalers

What is your standard MOQ for OEM air purifier orders?

Our standard MOQ is usually 500 units, depending on model, customization level and packaging requirements. Trial order discussion is available for serious brand projects.

Can you customize logo, color and packaging?

Yes. We support logo printing, private-label packaging, color matching, manual design, carton design and product display customization.

Can you customize the filter system?

Yes. We can discuss HEPA grade, activated carbon density, pre-filter design and filter replacement packaging according to your target market.

Do you support smart app or sensor functions?

Yes. Selected models can support smart app control, air-quality sensor, auto mode, timer, sleep mode and display-panel customization.

Which markets can you support?

We support global B2B buyers. Certification and document needs should be confirmed by destination market before mass production.
