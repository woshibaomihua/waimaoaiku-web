The Invisible Threat to Your Health: Toxic Indoor Air

"Did you know the air inside your home can be up to 100 times more polluted than outdoor air?"

What's Polluting Your Home's Air?

Modern homes are built for energy efficiency, effectively sealing us inside with a cocktail of invisible threats. We spend 90% of our lives indoors, breathing air trapped with PM2.5, microplastics from synthetic fabrics, toxic cooking fumes, and harmful gases (VOCs) released by furniture and cleaning supplies.

Hidden Health Threats of Indoor Air Pollution

The average person takes 20,000 breaths per day. When we inhale ultra-fine particles, they don't just stop at the lungs—they enter our bloodstream and circulate throughout the body.
