Custom OEM Filter Sourcing & Replenishment Programs

We custom-manufacture filters to fit any brand's physical dimensions and CADR requirements. Our capabilities include fabricating composite multi-layer structures (Pre-filter + True HEPA + Activated Carbon + Cold Catalyst) with secure rubber gasketing and custom-branded outer pull-tabs. Our dust-free filter lines ensure absolute structural integrity, prevent particulate bypass, and maximize filter lifespan.

  • Medical-Grade H13 HEPA: High-efficiency pleat designs capturing 99.97% of fine dust, wildfire ash, pollen, and airborne particles down to 0.3 microns.
  • High-Adsorption Activated Carbon: Choose from thin carbon sheets or heavy granular carbon beds to maximize odor and VOC capture.
  • Custom Dielines & Branding: Customize the filter's outer frame, add pull-tabs printed with your logo, and utilize custom-printed outer boxes.
  • Filter Subscription Planning: Establish a direct filter replenishment program to keep your distributors and retail customers continuously stocked.
