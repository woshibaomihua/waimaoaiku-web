HEPA H13 vs H14: What Air Purifier Buyers Should Know

Understand how HEPA H13 and H14 positioning affects air purifier OEM projects and product marketing.

In this guidePositioningMarketing clarityFilter supplyBuyer questionsRequest Quote

Positioning

HEPA H13 is widely used for home air purifier positioning, while H14 can be used for more premium or medical-grade storytelling when the product design supports it.

Marketing clarity

Avoid vague claims. Explain filter structure, replacement cycle and target use cases in a way buyers and consumers can understand.

Filter supply

Plan replacement filter SKUs from the beginning so the product line can generate repeat sales after the initial machine order.

Buyer questions

Professional buyers should ask for filter specification, product photos, packaging wording and sample confirmation before production.
