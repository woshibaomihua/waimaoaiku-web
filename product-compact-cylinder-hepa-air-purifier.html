Product Overview

Compact cylindrical design with a transparent body that lets buyers see the internal fan structure — ideal for visual differentiation on shelf. Bottom 360° air intake draws air evenly from all directions. H13 HEPA + activated carbon + pre-filter three-stage system captures PM2.5, pollen, smoke and odors. 5 fan speed levels cover quiet night use to strong airflow mode.
