Warranty Coverage

AERLUNE template products are presented with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects under normal household use. This is a template policy and must be adapted to your actual product quality agreement and legal requirements.

What is Covered

  • Electrical or mechanical defects caused by manufacturing issues
  • Display, fan or sensor issues not caused by misuse
  • Defects discovered during normal recommended usage

What is Not Covered

The policy template excludes misuse, accidental damage, unauthorized repairs, cosmetic wear, water damage, incorrect voltage use, consumable filters, commercial abuse and damage caused by shipping carriers after delivery confirmation.

How to Request Warranty Service

1Prepare order number, purchase proof and model name.
2Take clear photos or video showing the issue.
3Submit the support form and wait for review.
4Receive repair, replacement or troubleshooting instructions.

OEM / Wholesale Warranty Note

For distributors, private-label brands and OEM orders, warranty terms should be defined in the purchase contract, including defect rate, spare parts, replacement process and after-sales responsibility.
