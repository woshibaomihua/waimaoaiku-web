Return Window

The benchmark site uses a 30-day home-trial message. Before launch, define the exact return window based on your local law, sales channel, warehouse ability and product condition requirements.

Eligibility

  • Product must be returned with original accessories and packaging where possible
  • Serial number or order number may be required
  • Filters and consumables may be excluded if opened or used
  • Products damaged by misuse may not qualify

Return Process

1Contact support with order number and reason.
2Receive return authorization and shipping instructions.
3Ship the product back with required packaging.
4Refund is reviewed after warehouse inspection.

Refund Timing

Refund timing depends on the payment provider and bank. A practical template is to process approved refunds within 5–10 business days after return inspection.

Exchanges

Exchanges may be available for defective units, wrong items or model upgrades depending on stock availability and price differences.

Bulk / OEM Orders

Customized private-label, OEM packaging, logo-printing and bulk wholesale orders should not follow ordinary retail return rules. Define these terms in the sales contract.

Need help?
