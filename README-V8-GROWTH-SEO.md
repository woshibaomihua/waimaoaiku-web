# AERLUNE V8 Premium Growth + SEO Site

This version upgrades the AERLUNE air purifier independent site from a visual showcase into a growth-oriented SEO site.

## Major upgrades
- Premium homepage rebuilt around search intent, product discovery and B2B/OEM conversion.
- Product collection and detail pages now separate public benchmark references from final product claims.
- Added/expanded SEO pages for allergies, pets, smoke, bedroom, large room, CADR, room size, HEPA, carbon filters, replacement filters, private label manufacturing and wholesale supply.
- Added `performance-testing.html`, `verified-data.html`, `llms.txt`, updated `robots.txt`, and full real-domain `sitemap.xml`.
- Added `growth-v8.css` and optimized WebP images in `assets/images/optimized/`.
- Added JSON-LD structured data for Organization, WebSite, WebPage, ItemList, Product, Article and FAQPage where relevant.

## Important compliance note
AERLUNE is still a prototype brand site. Benchmark data is not a final product performance claim. Replace all benchmark references with actual third-party lab reports before commercial launch.
