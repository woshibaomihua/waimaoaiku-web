Processing Time

Most template retail orders are described as processing within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Actual processing time should be updated based on your warehouse, inventory and fulfillment workflow.

Shipping Fees

The site header currently uses “Free Shipping” as a conversion message. Before launch, replace this with your true threshold, eligible regions and excluded product types.

Estimated Delivery

  • Domestic standard delivery template: 3–7 business days
  • Remote-area delivery may take longer
  • Tracking information is sent after shipment
  • International retail shipping should be configured separately

Tracking

After your order ships, customers should receive a tracking number by email. For a static GitHub Pages site, connect tracking through Shopify, WooCommerce, 17TRACK, AfterShip or your order-management system.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If a package arrives damaged, customers should keep the packaging and send photos within the stated claim window. For lost packages, confirm carrier status before issuing replacement or refund decisions.

OEM / Bulk Shipping

OEM, ODM and wholesale orders should be quoted separately by Incoterms, destination port, shipping method, packaging size, carton quantity, plug type and certification needs.
