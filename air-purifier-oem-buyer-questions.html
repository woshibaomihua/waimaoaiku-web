3. What Certifications Are Required for Exporting Air Purifiers Internationally?

Compliance is non-negotiable for custom air purifiers. Different global markets have specific regulatory requirements:

  • USA Market: Requires FCC compliance for electrical emissions and CARB (California Air Resources Board) certification for zero-ozone standards. Brands may optionally require ETL or UL safety markings.
  • European Market: Mandatory CE marking (covering LVD safety, EMC, and RoHS substance restrictions) is required for selling in EU member states. Learn about filter efficiency standards in our article comparing HEPA H13 vs H14 filters.
  • Middle East & Other Regions: Often request SASO (Saudi Arabia) or regional G-Mark standards. Our standard factory models come equipped with CE, FCC, RoHS, and CARB, and we support custom certification projects on demand.

5. What is the Step-by-Step Bulk Production Workflow?

Direct B2B manufacturing follows a strict path to ensure 100% defect-free delivery. Our standard timeline takes 30–45 days:

  1. Inquiry & Consultation: Discuss model specifications, target markets, plug types, and packaging preferences.
  2. Sample Verification: We manufacture a working prototype for your physical quality and performance evaluation.
  3. Deposit & Design: Secure the order with a 30% deposit, and finalize all color box, label, and manual artwork.
  4. Pre-Production Sign-off: Approve the final pre-production sample before the assembly lines begin bulk output.
  5. Bulk Manufacturing: We initiate shell molding, PCB mounting, motor assembly, and continuous aging tests.
  6. Quality Auditing & Shipping: Every single unit undergoes rigorous visual and operational inspection. Upon balance payment clearance, we coordinate with your forwarder for port delivery.

8. What Sourcing Information Should Buyers Prepare Before Requesting a Quote?

To receive a highly accurate, factory-direct quotation within 24 hours, B2B buyers should provide our sales team with:

  1. Target Sourcing Market: Required for correct voltage, plug styles, and standard packaging certifications (e.g. EU vs US).
